The Challenge

Billing vs. Revenue:
The Great Disconnect

1

Old Rule: You bill $0, you book $0 revenue. Simple.

2

New Rule (Ind AS 115): Revenue is based on "Control," not your invoice date.

!

Result: You recognize revenue for the phone on Day 1, even if the bill says $0.

The Framework

The 5-Step Model

1. Identify the Contract with the customer.
2. Identify distinct Performance Obligations (PO).
3. Determine the Transaction Price (TTP).
4. Allocate TTP to POs based on Standalone Prices.
5. Recognize Revenue when control transfers.
